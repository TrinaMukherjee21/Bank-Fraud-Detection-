═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                           FRAUD RISK COMBINATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🟢 LOW RISK (Legitimate)
   ✓ Small-medium amounts ($10-$500)
   ✓ Normal hours (step 100-600)
   ✓ Common categories (grocery, gas, utilities)
   ✓ Established customers

🟡 MEDIUM RISK (Monitor)
   ⚠️ Higher amounts ($500-$5,000)
   ⚠️ Online or travel purchases
   ⚠️ Late hours (step 600-800)
   ⚠️ Younger customers

🟠 HIGH RISK (Review Required)
   🚨 Large amounts ($5,000-$15,000)
   🚨 Very late hours (step 800+)
   🚨 Cash withdrawals
   🚨 Round amounts ($5,000, $10,000)

🔴 VERY HIGH RISK (Fraud Alert)
   🔥 Extreme amounts ($15,000+)
   🔥 Multiple red flags
   🔥 Step 900+ with large amounts
   🔥 ATM + High amount + Late time
   🔥 Young customer + Very high amount
